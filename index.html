About

I'm Harpal Singh, and I use this blog to share what I'm learning and building in data engineering. I'm especially interested in pipelines, orchestration, warehouse design, data quality, and the practical work of making data systems easier to trust.

My goal here is to write clearly about real engineering problems: what breaks, what scales poorly, what teams usually overlook, and what design choices make platforms more reliable over time.
